Where we work

Gravity Construction Service Areas

We build and remodel throughout Santa Cruz County — from the Westside to the San Lorenzo Valley. Here's what to know about construction in each of the communities we serve.

Service area

Santa Cruz

Gravity Construction is a locally owned, licensed general contractor working throughout the City of Santa Cruz — from the Westside and Seabright to Pleasure Point, the Upper Westside, and the downtown neighborhoods around Mission Hill.

Learn more →

Service area

Capitola

Capitola's tight lots, historic charm, and literal oceanfront exposure make it one of the most rewarding — and least forgiving — places we build. We've done everything from Jewel Box cottage refreshes to Depot Hill whole-home remodels.

Learn more →

Service area

Aptos

From Rio del Mar oceanfronts to Aptos Hills ranches, we build and remodel across every corner of Aptos. Our clients here tend to be long-term owners making once-in-a-generation investments in their homes.

Learn more →

Service area

Soquel

Soquel sits at the crossroads of Santa Cruz County — close enough to the coast to feel the breeze, far enough inland to escape the fog. We remodel across the entire 95073 zip, from the Village to the top of Old San Jose Road.

Learn more →

Service area

Live Oak

Live Oak runs from the Santa Cruz city limit at Rodriguez Street down past Pleasure Point to the Capitola line. It's the densest unincorporated area in the county and the one where we see the most variety in our Monday schedule.

Learn more →

Service area

Scotts Valley

Scotts Valley is its own incorporated city with its own permit counter, its own design tastes, and a housing stock that ranges from 1970s planned developments to modern estate properties on the Glenwood ridge.

Learn more →

Service area

Ben Lomond

Ben Lomond and the rest of the San Lorenzo Valley are where we take on the county's most character-rich — and most challenging — remodels. Mountain homes, steep driveways, big redwoods, and post-2020-fire sensibilities.

Learn more →

Service area

Felton

Felton sits at the gateway to the San Lorenzo Valley — wooded, character-rich, and full of homes that deserve better than the minimum. We build and remodel throughout the 95018 zip, from Zayante to the edge of Henry Cowell.

Learn more →
