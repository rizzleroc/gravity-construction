Aptos is where Santa Cruz County gets serious about remodels

The median Aptos home has more square footage, more yard, and more deferred maintenance than its city-of-Santa-Cruz counterpart. Our Aptos clients usually aren't refreshing a single room — they're doing whole-home remodels, second-story additions, ADUs on the back of the lot, and kitchen-plus-primary-suite combos that take 4–6 months on site.

Aptos is unincorporated Santa Cruz County, so permits go through County Planning (701 Ocean Street) rather than a city. The county's ministerial ADU track is efficient; discretionary work like hillside additions takes longer because Geotechnical Review gets involved.

Neighborhoods we work in

  • Rio del Mar flats — Beach-adjacent tracts, 1960s–1980s construction, usually needing full electrical and plumbing upgrades.
  • Rio del Mar bluffs — Ocean-view homes where finish spec really matters; we've built several rear decks and outdoor kitchens here.
  • Seacliff — Historic cottages near Seacliff State Beach, narrow access, careful staging required.
  • Aptos Village & Trout Gulch — Remodels of homes that predate the Village redevelopment; character-rich but wiring-challenged.
  • Aptos Hills & Larsen Road — Larger properties, occasional septic and well work in coordination with our trade partners, barn-style ADUs and workshops.
  • Day Valley & Freedom Boulevard — Semi-rural parcels, agricultural overlays, ADU detached builds over 1,000 sq ft.

Whole-home remodels are our bread and butter in Aptos

A typical Aptos whole-home runs $400K–$900K with a 6–9 month timeline. We'll usually recommend doing electrical, plumbing, insulation, windows, HVAC, and the kitchen in one mobilization rather than spreading it across phases — it's 20–30% cheaper overall and the house comes back together as a cohesive system rather than a series of patches.

Hillside work and geotech

If your Aptos property has any slope — and most do — expect a geotechnical report in the permit package for additions or foundation work. We coordinate with local soils engineers we've used for years; they know the county's expectations and don't over-spec.

FAQ

Common questions — Aptos

Do you do whole-home remodels while we live in the house?

For remodels under about 40% of the home we can usually phase around you. For larger scopes we strongly recommend relocating — it saves money, shortens the schedule, and preserves your sanity.

How does the county ADU process work in Aptos?

Submit to County Planning, get a 60-day ministerial review for standard ADUs under 1,200 sq ft, then building permit and construction. Total from contract to move-in is typically 8–12 months.
