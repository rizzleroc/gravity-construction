General Contractor in Felton, CA

Felton sits at the gateway to the San Lorenzo Valley — wooded, character-rich, and full of homes that deserve better than the minimum. We build and remodel throughout the 95018 zip, from Zayante to the edge of Henry Cowell.

Start a Project Call (831) 234-4669

Felton homes have soul and require patience

A typical Felton remodel we take on is a 1950s or 1960s cabin-style home that's been loved by three generations and now needs a kitchen that functions, a bathroom that's waterproof, and exterior hardening against fire. We understand how to preserve what makes these homes special — the tongue-and-groove ceilings, the stone fireplaces, the redwood paneling — while quietly upgrading everything that matters behind the walls.

Felton is unincorporated Santa Cruz County. Much of it is mapped as WUI. Permits come through County Planning; the rebuild-streamlining program set up after the CZU fires has significantly sped up post-fire work.

Areas we cover

  • Zayante & East Zayante — Cabin remodels, ADU builds, fire hardening.
  • Lompico — Steep driveways, creek-proximity, careful staging.
  • Mount Hermon Road & Graham Hill edge — Larger lots, semi-custom remodels and additions.
  • Henry Cowell / Roaring Camp edge — Riparian-adjacent properties with extra environmental review.

What Felton needs from a contractor

A willingness to drive. Comfort with narrow roads. A subcontractor bench that doesn't flinch when the address is on an unpaved lane. Rigor about fire code. Respect for old-growth trees. We check all of those boxes.
