    Questions

    Frequently asked

    Is Gravity Construction licensed in California?

    Yes. Gravity Construction holds California State License Board (CSLB) License #1075110 and is fully insured and bonded. You can verify at cslb.ca.gov.

    What areas do you serve?

    All of Santa Cruz County — Santa Cruz, Capitola, Aptos, Soquel, Live Oak, Scotts Valley, Ben Lomond, and Felton.

    Do you offer free estimates?

    Yes. Every project starts with a free on-site consultation and a detailed written estimate with a clear scope of work — no vague line items.

    What services do you offer?

    New home construction, ADUs and home additions, whole-home remodels, kitchen and bath remodels, custom finish carpentry, commercial tenant improvements, and restaurant build-outs.

    Will the owner be on the job site?

    Yes. Owner Damien Hollinga supervises every project personally — we keep our workload small enough to stay hands-on through every phase.

    How long does a typical remodel take?

    Kitchen remodels typically run 6–10 weeks; whole-home remodels 3–6 months; new construction and ADUs vary by scope and permit timeline. We'll give you a realistic schedule in your estimate.
